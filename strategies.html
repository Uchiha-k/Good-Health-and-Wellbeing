Strengthening Health Systems

Enhancing healthcare infrastructure, training healthcare workers, ensuring the availability of essential medicines and medical supplies.

Promoting Preventive Healthcare Measures

Emphasizing preventive healthcare measures such as promoting healthy lifestyle behaviors and health education campaigns to prevent the onset of diseases and reduce the burden of illness.

Enhancing Access to Essential Medicines and Vaccines

Ensuring equitable access to essential medicines, vaccines, and other healthcare technologies by improving affordability, availability, and accessibility, particularly in low-resource settings

Investing in Maternal and Child Health

Prioritizing maternal and child health by ensuring access to prenatal care, skilled birth attendance, postnatal care, family planning services, and essential newborn care.

Addressing Social Determinants of Health

This involves implementing policies and interventions to address poverty, inequality, education, housing, employment, and environmental conditions to achieve health equity and improve population health.

Promoting Universal Health Coverage

Universal health coverage encompasses access to quality healthcare services, including promotion, prevention, treatment, rehabilitation, and palliative care, without discrimination.
