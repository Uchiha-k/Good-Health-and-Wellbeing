Get Involved

Raise Awareness

Educate yourself and others about the importance of good health and wellbeing. Share information about SDG 3 and its targets through advocacy campaigns and educational initiatives.

Advocate for Policy Change

Advocate for policies and initiatives that promote health equity, increase access to quality healthcare services, and address the social determinants of health. Support legislation and government programs that prioritize public health and wellbeing.

Support Health Organizations

Volunteer your time, skills, or resources to support local and international health organizations working to improve health outcomes and address health disparities. This could involve volunteering at a healthcare facility, participating in medical missions, or fundraising for health-related causes.

Promote Healthy Behaviors

Encourage healthy lifestyle choices within your community, such as regular exercise, balanced nutrition, tobacco cessation, and stress management, to promote better health and wellbeing.

Engage in Global Health Initiatives

Get involved in global health initiatives and partnerships that focus on addressing specific health challenges, such as infectious diseases, maternal and child health, or non-communicable diseases. Join advocacy campaigns, participate in events, and contribute to fundraising efforts.
